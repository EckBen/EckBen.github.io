Wind Speed Analysis

This figure shows the relationship between wind speed and latitude. Interestingly, this analysis shows that wind speed appears to decrease along with decreasing latitude. This is in contrast to the inverse “U”-shaped relationship seen in both the temperature analysis and humidity analysis. However, the data is not strongly correlated, there is a fairly clear "U" shaped pattern in this scatter plot with the valley of the "U" sitting near 10° latitude.
